About IDR

Where Academic Insight Meets Industry Reality

The Institute of Digital Risk is an industry-led training and deployment institute purpose-built for digital, cyber, and technology risk roles in regulated and high-consequence sectors. We exist to close a critical skills gap — producing practitioners who are not only theoretically grounded but operationally ready on day one.

IDR operates at the intersection of a leading UK university partnership and a growing network of industry clients. Our programmes leverage the latest in academic research on AI risk, cyber governance, and digital resilience — then translate that knowledge into real-world projects, live case studies, and direct deployment pathways into hiring organisations.

Design Rationale

Logo & Brand Notes

Symbolism: The isometric cube represents structure, layers, and resilience — the three visible faces mirror the multi-dimensional nature of risk (technical, organisational, human). The top face is orange, signifying leadership, foresight, and energy at the apex of the organisation; the darker side faces ground it in seriousness and precision.

Colour: Orange (#F46012) is chosen for its connotations of alertness, urgency, and innovation — qualities central to risk management — without the negativity of red. Black and near-black grounds the palette with authority, suited to high-consequence sectors such as financial services and critical infrastructure.

Typography: Barlow Condensed (display) brings industrial strength and technical precision — its tall letterforms work at large scales and reduce well to small sizes. Outfit (body) provides clean, modern legibility at reading sizes, balancing the bold headlines with openness.

Scalability: The cube icon strips to a clear 16×16 favicon silhouette; the full-lockup variant separates the IDR monogram from the stacked wordmark for immediate brand recognition at mobile header dimensions.
